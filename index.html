Claire Sinagra About Claire lives in the Rocky Mountains with her husband, an exceptionally perfect dog, and an attack cat. Books Claire’s books have received starred reviews in Publishers Weekly, Library Journal, and Booklist. She is a New York Times and a USA Today bestseller and a RITA® winner.
